About Lumina Studio

Founded in 2010, Lumina Studio has been capturing life's most precious moments for over a decade. Our team of passionate photographers brings creativity, technical expertise, and a keen eye for detail to every session.

We believe that every photograph tells a story, and we're dedicated to helping you tell yours in the most beautiful way possible. From intimate portraits to grand celebrations, we approach each project with the same level of care and commitment.

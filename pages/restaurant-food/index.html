Our Story

A Culinary Journey Started in 2010

What began as a small family dream has blossomed into one of the city's most beloved dining destinations. Our founder, Chef Marcus Chen, brought his passion for authentic flavors and innovative techniques from Paris to create something truly special.

Today, we continue that legacy with a team of passionate culinary artists who share the same vision: to create unforgettable dining experiences that nourish both body and soul.

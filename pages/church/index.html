About Grace Community Church

Founded in 1985, Grace Community Church has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community for over three decades. We believe in the transformative power of God's love and the importance of living it out together.

Our mission is simple: to help people know God, find freedom, discover purpose, and make a difference. We're not perfect, but we're pursuing Jesus together, and we'd love for you to join us on that journey.

Whether you're new to faith, have been walking with God for years, or are simply curious about what church is all about, there's a place for you here.

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

How does the escrow system work?
When you make a purchase, your payment is held securely in escrow. The seller is notified to ship the item. Once you receive and confirm the item matches the description, funds are released to the seller. If there's an issue, we hold the funds until it's resolved.

Is MarketHub really free to use?
Yes! MarketHub is completely free with zero listing fees, zero transaction fees, and no hidden charges. Sellers keep 100% of their earnings, and buyers pay exactly the listed price.

How are sellers verified?
All sellers undergo a multi-step verification process including government ID verification, email and phone number confirmation, and a review of their selling history. Verified sellers receive a badge on their profile.

What if I have a dispute with a seller?
Our dedicated dispute resolution team is available 24/7. Simply open a dispute from your order page, and we'll mediate between you and the seller. Since funds are held in escrow, your money is protected until the issue is resolved.

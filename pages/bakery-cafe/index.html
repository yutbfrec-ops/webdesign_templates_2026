Our Story

Baking with Passion
Since 2009

What started as a small family kitchen has grown into a beloved neighborhood institution. Our founder, Maria Chen, brought her grandmother's recipes from Italy and added her own modern twist.

Every morning at 3 AM, our bakers begin crafting each item by hand. We source our flour from local mills, our butter from family farms, and our coffee beans directly from sustainable growers.
